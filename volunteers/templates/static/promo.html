FOSDEM
Volunteering

This is a site that brings all the volunteers together. Find all the cool tasks that you are interested in.

Bug reports welcome at Github.

  • FOSDEM

    FOSDEM is a free event that offers open source communities a place to meet, share ideas and collaborate.

  • Location

    FOSDEM will take place at the ULB Solbosch Campus, Brussels, Belgium, Europe, Earth.

  • Volunteering

    FOSDEM needs you! Every year, the FOSDEM team is assisted by an enthusiastic team of volunteers to help out with various tasks and make the event a fun and safe place for all our visitors.

  • Mailing List

    You can subscribe to receive updates from the mailing list

    You can post a message to all the members, send email to volunteers@lists.fosdem.org

  • IRC

    IRC is available in #fosdem-volunteers (webchat) on freenode
